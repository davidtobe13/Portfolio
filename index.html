About

More About Me

I am David Tobechukwu Ugwuegbulam. I am a graphic designer, UI/UX designer and Front-end web developer based in Lagos, Nigeria.

Howdy!

I currently work remotely as a Senior graphic designer at Jace prints Nigeria where I design all sort of graphic works which includes branding, advert flyers, Logo design, designs for banners and more. I also work remotely as a social media graphic designer for The Page handler, where I create designs meant for adverts on social media platforms. Having spent a few years mastering Front-end web development and UI/UX Design and still working on perfection, I've not had any formal opportunity to make my skill effective, but will do well to leave a few of my personal projects in my portfolio.

I have an Ordinary National Diploma (OND) in Computer Science from Federal Polytechnic Nekede, Owerri with Higher National Diploma in progress in same Institution. My non-tech activities include photography, basketball and playing video games.
